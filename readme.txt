== Description ==

Chosen is a bold and minimalist theme for publishers who want to get their visitors' attention and keep it. Present your best content in a simple, uncluttered fashion on phones, tablets, and desktop computers with Chosen. Use Chosen to start a blog, magazine, or news site in any niche: fashion, business, gaming, food, travel. As a free theme, you can use Chosen for as many sites as you want. It can even be used for video, podcasts, and personal resumes. Now with WooCommerce support, you can also add an eCommerce store to your website.

**Live Demo**

View a fully-setup live demo of Chosen here: [Live demo](https://www.competethemes.com/demos/?theme=chosen)

**Features**

Chosen comes with the following features:

* Custom logo
* Social media icons
* Show full posts or experts
* Custom excerpt length
* Make first post on blog extra wide

**Chosen Pro**

Chosen also has an upgrade called [Chosen Pro](https://www.competethemes.com/chosen-pro/) that comes with the following features:

* Custom colors
* Featured sliders
* Featured videos
* Additional layouts
* Flexible header image
* Google fonts
* More widget areas
* Display controls
* Custom footer text
